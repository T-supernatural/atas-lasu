About A.T.A.S - L.A.S.U

We are the heartbeat of theatre arts students at Lagos State University, fostering creativity, community, and craft. Dive into our story, mission, and vision.

Our Story

Founded by the 23rd Atas lasu Executive - Director D LED Administration, also with the great passionate students and alumni, A.T.A.S is more than an association — it’s a movement. We unite theatre artists to collaborate, innovate, and elevate the craft on and off the stage.

Over the years, we've built a vibrant community of creatives who push boundaries, inspire audiences, and support one another. Join us as we continue this journey of artistic excellence.

Our Mission

To nurture the creative talents of every theatre art student at LASU, offering a platform that encourages expression, growth, and collaboration — both academically and artistically.

Our Vision

To be the most dynamic, inclusive, and impactful student arts association in Nigeria — where every voice matters, every story counts, and theatre becomes a tool for change.

Our Core Values

At the heart of ATAS, we believe in fostering growth, community, and creativity.

Creativity

We celebrate the power of imagination in every performance and project.

Community

Together, we support each other’s journeys and build lasting bonds.

Discipline

Excellence on and off the stage comes from commitment and hard work.

Innovation

We’re not afraid to break boundaries and redefine theatre for a new generation.

Integrity

We stand by our values and keep it real — on stage and in life.

Leadership

We empower students to lead change and take initiative.
